Supermarkets

Albert Heijn

Albert Heijn, the largest supermarket chain in the Netherlands, has a location in Haarlem's Overveen district, offering a wide range of goods, including fresh produce, meats, and household items. Known for high-quality products and excellent service, it attracts both locals and internationals. The store also features the AH Bonus Card for weekly discounts, self-checkout options for convenience, and a focus on sustainability with organic products and eco-friendly packaging.

Vomar

Vomar Supermarket in Haarlem is a popular and affordable grocery chain offering fresh produce, bakery items, meats, and household goods. Known for its convenient locations and customer-focused approach, Vomar provides regular promotions and discounts while maintaining quality products. With friendly staff and well-organized aisles, it offers a pleasant shopping experience, making it a go-to spot for local residents seeking efficient grocery shopping.

Action

Action Supermarket in Haarlem is a popular discount store offering a wide selection of affordable household products, personal care items, and groceries. Known for its low prices and constantly changing stock, it attracts budget-conscious shoppers looking for everyday essentials. With frequent deals and discounted prices, Action has become a go-to spot for practical and affordable shopping in Haarlem.
