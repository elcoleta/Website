Transportation

NS

Before traveling to the Netherlands, download the NS (Nederlandse Spoorwegen) transportation app on your phone. This is because it's necessary to use the Dutch rail system. Essential Elements of the NS App: Train Timetables and Up-to-Date Information: Get up to date information on platform changes, travel interruptions, and train schedules. Purchasing and Managing Tickets: Use the app to purchase and manage tickets, including travel passes and single-journey tickets. Travel Planner: Determine the fastest paths between locations and learn how train lines connect. Route Notifications: Create alerts to get notifications on changes for particular routes. Using Haarlem's NS App: Making Links: Find rail alternatives to and from Haarlem with ease, including connections to important destinations like Rotterdam, Utrecht, and Amsterdam. Checking journey Times: For trip planning purposes, obtain precise journey times. Advantages for Upcoming Foreign Students: Ease of Use: An intuitive UI made for beginners. Language Support: Several languages are supported for simpler navigation. Transport Integration: Bus and tram information to assist you go around Haarlem and beyond. Installing the NS app ahead of time will, in general, make your adjustment to Dutch living easier and increase your comfort level with the public transport network.

Student Card

In Haarlem, the Netherlands, the student card is a vital identity document for students, offering a range of savings on local services, cultural events, and transit. You must be enrolled in a Haarlem-based educational facility, such as a university or technical college, in order to receive the student card. If your school does not automatically provide student cards at the start of the academic year, you can apply through the student services department of your establishment. Generally, you'll need to present documentation that is current (like a passport or ID card), proof of enrollment (such a student registration certificate), and a recent passport-sized photo if needed. You could have to pick up your card in person or receive it by mail, depending on the institution. The student card has several advantages, such as reduced rates on local and NS train tickets, entry fees to museums and cultural events, and exclusive deals at participating stores, eateries, and cafes. All things considered, having a student card in Haarlem improves your time as a student by offering more discounts and advantages!

Scooters

On the other hand, for students looking for a faster way to get around, renting a scooter is a great option. Felyx and GoSharing are two popular electric scooter-sharing services in Haarlem, providing a quick and eco-friendly way to travel. With both services, you can easily rent a scooter through their mobile apps, locate one nearby, and start riding. However, it’s important to note that a valid driving license is required to rent a scooter. Both companies offer flexible pay-per-minute pricing, making it an affordable choice for students who need transportation for short trips around the city or even nearby areas. For more details and to sign up, you can visit their websites: Felyx and GoSharing.
