Activities

Patronaat

Patronaat is Haarlem’s iconic live music venue, known for its diverse lineup and vibrant atmosphere. Located in the heart of the city, Patronaat hosts a wide range of events, from local indie bands to international artists, spanning genres like rock, electronic, hip-hop, and more. With its cutting-edge sound systems and intimate setting, it’s a favorite spot for both music lovers and performers. Whether you're looking to discover new music or enjoy a night out with friends, Patronaat offers an unforgettable experience that reflects the lively cultural pulse of Haarlem. It's a must-visit for students who want to dive into the city's energetic music scene.

Pathe cinema

Pathé Haarlem is the city’s premier cinema, offering a modern movie-going experience in a comfortable and stylish setting. Located near the city center, Pathé features the latest blockbuster films, indie releases, and special screenings in multiple languages, making it a popular destination for both locals and international students. With its state-of-the-art sound and visual technology, including IMAX, Pathé ensures an immersive cinematic experience. Whether you're in the mood for action, drama, or a classic film, Pathé Haarlem provides a perfect escape into the world of movies, complete with cozy seating and a range of snacks to enjoy during the show.

Library

The library in Haarlem, known as the Bibliotheek Zuid-Kennemerland, is a vibrant cultural hub that offers a wide range of resources and services to the community. Located in the heart of the city, it provides a welcoming space for reading, learning, and socializing. The library boasts an extensive collection of books, magazines, and digital resources, catering to all age groups and interests. It also hosts various workshops, events, and educational programs, making it a vital part of Haarlem's cultural and intellectual life. The modern and spacious facility ensures a calm environment for study and relaxation
